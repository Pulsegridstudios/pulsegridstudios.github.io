The Game

One Reactor.
Infinite Pressure.

Looped Operation puts you in charge of a real Advanced Gas-cooled Reactor. Monitor live telemetry, manage control rod insertion, regulate coolant flow, and respond to emergencies — all within Roblox.

Built by PulseGrid Studios with obsessive attention to reactor physics. Currently in active development with Unit 1 as the sole operational unit.
