Privacy Policy — Looped Operations Discord Bot

Last updated: April 2026

1. Overview

The Looped Operations Discord Bot ("the Bot") is designed to integrate Discord with the Looped Operations game, providing moderation tools, logging, and gameplay-related features. We are committed to respecting your privacy and handling data responsibly.

2. Information We Collect

The Bot may collect and process the following data:

Discord Data

  • User IDs
  • Server (Guild) IDs
  • Channel IDs
  • Role IDs
  • Messages, only when required for moderation or logging features

Game Integration Data

  • Roblox user IDs
  • Rank or role information
  • In-game actions relevant to moderation or logging

System Data

  • Command usage
  • Error logs
  • Interaction timestamps

3. How We Use Data

We use collected data strictly for:

  • Linking Discord users to in-game accounts
  • Moderation and administrative tools
  • Logging actions such as warnings, bans, and system events
  • Improving bot functionality and stability

We do not use your data for advertising or profiling.

4. Data Storage

  • Data may be stored securely in databases or logging systems
  • Access is restricted to authorised developers only
  • We retain data only as long as necessary for functionality

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties.

Data may only be shared:

  • If required by law
  • To protect the integrity of the service
  • To prevent abuse or misuse

6. User Rights

You may request:

  • Access to your stored data
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your data, where applicable

To request this, contact the development team.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data, including:

  • Restricted access
  • Secure storage practices
  • Minimised data collection

However, no system is completely secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy at any time. Continued use of the Bot means you accept any changes.
